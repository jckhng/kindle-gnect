Exact Four in a Row
============

Unofficial Kindle-friendly Gnect/Connect Four game derived from GNOME Games ideas,
with a Kindle-specific GTK2/Cairo interface.

Project lineage
---------------

Exact Four in a Row is an unofficial Kindle-focused derivative/adaptation of GNOME
Games' Gnect. Original GNOME Games / Gnect code, ideas, and artwork remain
credited to their upstream authors. Kindle porting groundwork and packaging
references are credited to GnomeGames4Kindle, originally by crazy-electron, and later contributors.

This package is not an official GNOME project or an official GnomeGames4Kindle
release.
